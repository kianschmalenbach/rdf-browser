About: Maasgouw

An Entity of Type : List of municipalities of the Netherlands, from Named Graph : http://dbpedia.org, within Data Space : dbpedia.org

Maasgouw is a municipality in the Dutch province of Limburg. It is located on both banks of the river Meuse southwest of the city of Roermond. It was formed in a merger of the former municipalities of Heel, Maasbracht and Thorn on 1 January 2007. The municipality contains a number of towns and villages: * Beegden * Brachterbeek * Heel * Linne * Maasbracht * Ohé en Laak * Panheel * Stevensweert * Thorn * Wessem.
